  <p class="lead">
    Led by <strong>Dr. Mohamed Fathi Abdallah</strong><br>
    Assistant Professor of Food Toxicology<br>
    Department of Food Science, Aarhus University, Denmark
  </p>

  <p>
    Our research focuses on natural toxins (mycotoxins and cyanotoxins),
    emerging contaminants, and mixture toxicology in novel food.
    We combine LC-MS/MS approaches with advanced <em>in vitro</em> models
    to support risk assessment and food safety.
  </p>

  <p class="notice">
    We are currently accepting Master’s and PhD students.<br>
    Scholarships &amp; fellowship applications (Marie Curie, DFF, Novo Nordisk,
    Villum Foundation, and others) are actively supported.
  </p>

  <p>
    Contact: <a href="mailto:mfa@food.au.dk">mfa@food.au.dk</a>
  </p>
</header>
