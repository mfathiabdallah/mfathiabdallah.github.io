  <h1 style="margin: 0; font-size: 2.2em;">AU FOOD TOX Research Group</h1>
  <p style="font-size: 1.1em; max-width: 800px; margin: 15px auto 0;">
    Advancing food toxicology through cutting-edge research on natural toxins, emerging contaminants, and mixture risk assessment.
  </p>
</div>

<p>At AU FOOD Department, our research is centered on understanding the risks associated with natural toxins (mycotoxins and cyanotoxins) and other emerging contaminants in novel food. This is done by applying LC-MS/MS methods for detection. Through the use of in vitro models, we investigate the effects of these toxins as mixtures on human health. This interdisciplinary approach bridges food toxicology and human health, facilitating a comprehensive understanding of complex challenges.</p>
<p>For more details about my CV visit: <a href="https://www.mfathiabdallah.com/CV/" target="_blank">https://www.mfathiabdallah.com/CV/</a></p>
